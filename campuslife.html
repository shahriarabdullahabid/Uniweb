Campus Life

Jahangirnagar University Campus

Jahangirnagar University, located in Savar, near Dhaka, Bangladesh, is the country's first fully residential public university. Established in 1970, it offers a blend of academic excellence and natural beauty. The campus spans over 700 acres of lush greenery, featuring scenic lakes, vast open spaces, and peaceful surroundings that provide an ideal setting for academic and personal growth.

As a vibrant and dynamic institution, Jahangirnagar University emphasizes quality education and a rich cultural atmosphere. The university hosts various cultural, academic, and sports events throughout the year, making it a lively community for students and staff. Its unique campus provides not only educational resources but also a supportive environment for personal development and well-being.

Campus Facilities

Central Library

The JU library is well-stocked with academic resources, including books, journals, and e-resources.

Computer Lab

Our campus features a sports complex with indoor and outdoor facilities, including a gym, basketball courts, and a football field.

Central Field

Our university offers several playgrounds equipped for a variety of sports, including cricket, football, and basketball, providing ample space for recreation and team activities.

Salim-Al-Din Muktomoncho

Salim-Al-Din Muktomoncho is an open-air stage where various cultural events are staged throughout the year, offering a platform for students to showcase their talents and creativity.

Gymnasium

The gymnasium at Jahangirnagar University is well-equipped, allowing students and faculty to maintain their fitness through various exercise routines and activities.

Swimming Pool

The university features a well-maintained swimming pool that offers both relaxation and fitness opportunities for students, faculty, and staff.

Cafeteria

Enjoy a variety of food options at our cafeteria, offering both local and international cuisines for students and faculty.

Accommodation

Jahangirnagar University (JU) provides full residential facilities for both male and female students, with well-equipped hostels offering rooms, study spaces, dining options, and 24/7 security. The campus fosters a strong community, with recreational activities and Wi-Fi for a comfortable stay.

Medical Center

Our medical center is equipped to handle any health concerns. Our doctors and nurses are available to provide care and support to all students.

Transportation

Convenient campus transportation options, including shuttles and bike-sharing programs, ensure easy and safe travel across the university.

Research Center

Wazed Miah Science Research Centre (WMSRC) is recognized as the centre of excellence of Jahangirnagar University to provide scientific research facilities for researchers of JU. We also provide instrument facilities to other universities as well as research institutes and industries of Bangladesh. WMSRC is taking a leading role in experimentally probing scientific research at its most fundamental level.

Students Counselling and Guidance Centre

Our counseling center provides emotional and psychological support, helping students with personal and academic challenges to thrive during their time at university.

The Language Centre

The Language Centre offers language courses and workshops to enhance communication skills and cultural understanding for both local and international students.

Teacher-Student Centre

This center facilitates communication and collaboration between students and faculty members, offering resources for academic development and mentorship.

Special Needs Education Centre (SNEC)

SNEC provides tailored support and resources to students with special needs, ensuring an inclusive educational environment for all learners.

Center for Human Resource Development (CHRD)

CHRD focuses on enhancing the skills and capabilities of students and staff through training programs and workshops aimed at career development and personal growth.

About IBA-JU

The Institute of Business Administration, Jahangirnagar University (IBA-JU) is a pioneer in business education in Bangladesh, offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs that prepare students for global success. Renowned for its academic excellence and vibrant campus life, IBA-JU is dedicated to fostering leadership, innovation, and professional excellence.

About IBA - Jahangirnagar University

The Institute of Business Administration at Jahangirnagar University (IBA-JU) is one of the leading academic institutions in Bangladesh, renowned for its excellence in business education. Established in 1991, it is the pioneer of the Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program in the country. With modern facilities and a focus on innovation, IBA-JU graduates are shaping the future with skill and professionalism.
